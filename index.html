Scotland is a country placed in the UK, its next to Ireland and England.
It was founded in the 9th-10th century, by Kenneth MacAlpine.
It has around 5.51 million people in its population at this moment (2024).
The main four languages spoken here are Scots, Scottish Gaelic, English, and British sign language.
